This Mod adds 3 Widgets. The Position and Visibility of each Widget can be changed in the Config.

[i:2177] [c/ffff00:Current Ammo]

Shows your held Weapon's Ammo's Sprite, Name, Amount and Rarity. Only Visible when the Weapon has Ammo.

- Follows the Player. Can be turned off in the Config.
- Change its Size in the Config.

[i:493] [c/ffff00:Flight Time Bar]

Shows a Bar representing your Flight Time. Only visible when in Flight.

- Follows the Player. Can be turned off in the Config.

[i:2999] [c/ffff00:Minion Utilization]

Shows your current as well as your maximum Amount of Minions. No more spamming your Summon Weapon to make sure you have enough Minions.
